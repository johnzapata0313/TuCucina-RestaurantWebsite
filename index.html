Our Story

Los Arrieros Restaurant was born from a deep love of Colombian culture and the hearty, soulful flavors of the Antioquia region. Founded in 2012 by the Restrepo family, we set out to recreate the warm, welcoming atmosphere of a Colombian home kitchen right here in Philadelphia.

Every dish on our menu is prepared from scratch using traditional recipes and fresh, high-quality ingredients. From our slow-cooked frijoles to our crispy chicharrón, every bite tells a story of heritage and passion.
